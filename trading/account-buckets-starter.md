# X Account Buckets — Starter Version

This is a practical starter structure for building your X/Twitter trading intelligence system.
Do not add accounts just because they are famous.
Add them only if they improve decision quality.

## How To Judge An Account
Before adding any account, ask:
1. Does this account improve my market context?
2. Does it improve setup quality or just entertainment?
3. Is it early signal, confirmation, or noise?
4. Does it help me trade better or just feel informed?

---

## 1. Macro Bucket
Use for:
- rates
- dollar/liquidity regime
- recession/growth tone
- Fed / macro reaction framing
- risk-on vs risk-off context

Good account traits:
- explains macro clearly
- tracks liquidity / policy / rates
- does not rely on shock-posting
- helps explain market regime changes

What Yue should extract:
- market regime
- liquidity tone
- risk-on / risk-off implication
- macro event sensitivity

---

## 2. BTC / ETH Core Bucket
Use for:
- high-level crypto regime
- leadership quality
- whether BTC/ETH are confirming or rejecting alt risk
- market structure quality

Good account traits:
- focuses on structure, flows, or regime
- not endless moonboy posting
- useful for leadership confirmation

What Yue should extract:
- whether BTC or ETH is leading
- whether alt participation is healthy
- whether crypto beta is expanding or compressing

---

## 3. AI / High-Beta Crypto Bucket
Use for:
- AI token narrative
- leadership within AI names
- whether a theme is broad or concentrated in one leader
- second-order opportunities after leader strength

Good account traits:
- covers AI ecosystem seriously
- discusses TAO / FET / RNDR type themes with some substance
- not pure bag-pumping

What Yue should extract:
- dominant AI narrative
- leader vs laggard names
- crowding risk
- pullback vs chase conditions

---

## 4. Alt Rotation Bucket
Use for:
- whether capital is rotating beyond majors
- which sectors are being rewarded
- early changes in alt participation

Good account traits:
- talks in sector/rotation terms
- notices leadership shifts early
- can separate beta expansion from random pumps

What Yue should extract:
- where money is rotating into
- where money is rotating out of
- strongest alt sector
- weakest alt sector

---

## 5. Flow / Smart Money Bucket
Use for:
- ETF / exchange / on-chain / large-wallet framing
- institutional or whale-like positioning clues
- whether crowd narrative is backed by real movement

Good account traits:
- provides evidence, not just flexing
- tracks flows or positioning with context
- helps distinguish signal from hype

What Yue should extract:
- whether smart-money-style behavior aligns with X crowd
- whether there is divergence
- whether narratives are supported by flows

---

## 6. Event / Catalyst Bucket
Use for:
- earnings
- Fed / CPI / macro releases
- protocol upgrades
- token unlocks
- ETF or regulatory events

Good account traits:
- fast and reliable
- event-oriented, not opinion-heavy
- useful for timing risk

What Yue should extract:
- what event matters next
- how close it is
- whether it increases no-trade probability

---

## 7. Sentiment / Crowd Bucket
Use for:
- detecting overheating
- meme/fomo conditions
- euphoria / despair extremes
- narrative exhaustion

Good account traits:
- captures crowd mood clearly
- helpful as contrarian input
- not just random engagement bait

What Yue should extract:
- whether crowd is euphoric, confused, or fearful
- whether a narrative is too crowded
- whether price-action confirms the excitement

---

## Suggested Build Order
Do not build everything at once.
Start with:
1. Macro Bucket
2. BTC / ETH Core Bucket
3. AI / High-Beta Crypto Bucket
4. Event / Catalyst Bucket

Then add:
5. Alt Rotation Bucket
6. Flow / Smart Money Bucket
7. Sentiment / Crowd Bucket

---

## Best Practice
Keep each bucket small.
5 to 15 good accounts is better than 100 noisy ones.

## Rule For Yue
When reviewing X:
- summarize by bucket
- do not just summarize by account
- bucket summary is more useful than individual tweet summary
