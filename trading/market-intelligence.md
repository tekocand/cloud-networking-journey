# Market Intelligence Framework

## Purpose
This layer helps the trading advisor understand what is driving the market before discussing any specific trade.

The goal is not prediction.
The goal is context.

## Core Questions
Before evaluating setups, answer:
1. What narrative is driving this market right now?
2. Is money rotating into or out of this sector/theme?
3. What is the current sentiment regime?
4. Is there event risk that can invalidate technical setups?
5. Is smart money behavior aligned or divergent from retail behavior?

## Analysis Layers
### 1. Narrative Analysis
Identify the dominant story behind the move.
Examples:
- AI resurgence
- rate-cut pricing
- ETF flows
- sector rotation into defensives
- crypto beta rotation
- earnings repricing

Required output:
- dominant narrative
- secondary narrative
- catalyst
- likely duration: intraday / days / weeks / months
- contradiction risk

### 2. Sector / Theme Rotation
Track where money is flowing.
For each review, classify:
- money flowing into
- money flowing out of
- leaders
- laggards
- whether rotation supports the setup or weakens it

### 3. Sentiment Regime
Classify sentiment as:
- risk-off
- cautious
- balanced
- risk-on
- euphoric

Also say whether sentiment is:
- confirming trend
- stretched
- contrarian-warning

### 4. Smart Money Alignment
If data exists, check:
- institutional positioning
- whale accumulation/distribution
- insider activity
- exchange inflows/outflows
- flow divergence vs retail narrative

If data is missing, say missing.
Do not fake it.

### 5. Event Risk
Always identify upcoming event risks if relevant:
- macro data
- central bank decisions
- earnings
- protocol upgrades
- token unlocks
- ETF decisions
- major legal/regulatory events

## Output Structure For Market Intelligence
1. Dominant narrative
2. Secondary narrative
3. Sector / theme rotation
4. Sentiment regime
5. Smart money alignment
6. Event risk
7. What the market is probably rewarding
8. What the market is probably punishing
9. Implication for trading style
10. Key warning

## Implication Rules
The advisor must explicitly state whether the environment currently favors:
- breakout continuation
- pullback entries
- mean reversion
- lower size / lower frequency
- no trade

## Hard Rule
No specific trade setup should be rated highly if market context and narrative strongly contradict it.
