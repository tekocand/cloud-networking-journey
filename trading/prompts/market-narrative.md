# Market Narrative Prompt

Use this before screening or setup analysis.

## Prompt
Act as a disciplined market intelligence analyst.
Explain what is driving this market right now.
Do not pretend certainty.
If data is missing, state that clearly.

Inputs:
- Market:
- Timeframe of interest:
- Recent price behavior:
- Relevant news / events:
- Sector / theme context:
- Flow / smart money notes:

Output exactly in this structure:
1. Dominant narrative
2. Secondary narrative
3. Sector / theme rotation
4. Sentiment regime
5. Smart money alignment
6. Event risk
7. What the market is rewarding
8. What the market is punishing
9. Best-fit trading style right now
10. Contrarian warning

Rules:
- No fake precision.
- No unsupported macro claims.
- If event context is unclear, say unclear.
- End with whether the environment favors breakout / pullback / mean reversion / wait.
